# GST_Tools: Compliance & Logic Engines

An exclusive repository of web-based logic engines designed for GST compliance, tax determination, and audit accuracy.

## 🛠️ Included Tools

### 1. Place of Supply (POS) Engine
* **Purpose:** Determine whether a transaction attracts IGST or CGST/SGST based on the IGST Act, 2017.
* **Capabilities:** Handles both Goods (Section 10 & 11) and Services (Section 12) scenarios, including B2B and B2C status determination.

### 2. Value of Supply (VOS) Engine
* **Purpose:** Calculate the taxable value of goods and services as per Section 15 and Chapter IV Valuation Rules.
* **Capabilities:** Supports complex scenarios including Air Travel Agents (Rule 32(3)), Life Insurance (Rule 32(4)), Second-Hand Goods (Margin Scheme), and non-monetary consideration.

### 3. Time of Supply (TOS) Engine
* **Purpose:** Calculate the exact point in time when GST liability arises.
* **Capabilities:** Automates logic for Forward/Reverse Charge Mechanisms for both Goods and Services under Section 12 & 13 of the CGST Act.

## 🚀 Key Benefits
* **Accuracy:** Built on strict adherence to the Income Tax and GST Regulations, 2017.
* **Efficiency:** Designed for Chartered Accountants and tax professionals to reduce manual calculation errors during audits.
* **Accessibility:** Lightweight, offline-capable HTML tools with modern, responsive interfaces (Tailwind CSS/Vanilla JS).

## 💻 Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript.
* **Architecture:** Static logic engines requiring no server-side backend, ensuring client data privacy.
